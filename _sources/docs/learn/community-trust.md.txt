# Terra Community Trust

## FAQ

### What is a trust?

- A trust is a fiduciary relationship in which the trustees hold assets for the beneficiaries of the trust. The trustees must manage the assets as directed in the trust instrument and in accordance with the purposes of the trust.

### What assets will be held and controlled by the Terra Community Trust?

- The Terra Community Trust will hold all of the intellectual property rights to TERRA, LUNA, and UST.

### Who will be the beneficiaries of the Terra Community Trust?

- Each member of the Terra Community is a beneficiary of the Terra Community Trust.

### Who will control the Terra Community Trust?

- Through community proposals, each member of the Terra Community will have the opportunity to control the trust.

- The trust and trustees will be managed consistently with the governance protocol of the Terra Blockchain and, with a few exceptions (i.e. no fraud or illegal or unethical actions), the trustees must manage the assets as directed by the Terra Community through its community proposals.

- If directed by the community, the trust will have the ability to buy and sell property and enter into contracts, among other rights and privileges.

### What will be the purpose of the Terra Community Trust?

- The Terra Community Trust and its trustees must execute and carry out community-approved proposals to the extent those proposals relate to the assets held by the trust.

### Who will be the trustees of the Terra Community Trust?

- **The initial trustees of the Terra Community Trust will be Ryan Moore and Remi Tetot, and the initial enforcer of the trust will be Gabriel Shapiro; however, the community may vote to replace any one of these individuals at any time.**

### What is a fiduciary relationship?

- A fiduciary relationship is one in which one party (here, the trustees) owes the other party (here, the Terra Community) a fiduciary duty.
- In general, a fiduciary duty includes the duty of loyalty and the duty of care. The duty of loyalty implies that the trustee will always act in the best interests of the beneficiaries. The duty of care suggests that the trustees will not shirk their responsibilities or fall on the job.

### What will happen to the assets if the Terra Community Trust is terminated?

- The fate of the Terra Community Trust and its assets will be in the hands of the Terra Community. If the community votes to terminate the trust, the trust will terminate, and the assets will be distributed as voted upon by the community.
